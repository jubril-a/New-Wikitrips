GRE

Graduate Record ExaminationThe GRE is a standardized test often required for admission to graduate schools, primarily in the United States and some other countries. It's designed to measure general academic ability and includes sections that test verbal reasoning, quantitative reasoning, and analytical writing.

About the GRE

The Graduate Record Examination, or GRE, is an important step in the graduate school or business school application process. The GRE is a multiple-choice, computer-based, standardized exam that is often required for admission to graduate programs and graduate business programs (MBA) globally.

For further information about the GRE exam, please visit: www.ets.org/gre
